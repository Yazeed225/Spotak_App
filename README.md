# Spotak - Work & Study Cafe Finder


## Platfrom Overview

The platform enables users to locate study and work spaces through its cafe finder function. 
The web application serves as a business tool which assists students and freelancers
in finding their ideal study and remote work spaces. 


## Business Problem

Students and professionals need to locate cafes and workspaces which provide essential requirements for their 
productivity needs particularly high-speed Wi-Fi and silent spaces and power outlet access.
The standard map applications fail to offer users work-related amenity filters which results in their users 
spending excessive time on map searches while facing difficulties in their search. 


## Solution

involves creating a web-based directory system which enables users to assess and categorize 
local cafes according to their level of suitability for working. Users can use the application to search for specific
amenities while accessing community reviews which evaluate study spaces and they can book dedicated meeting rooms and 
study rooms through the application. 


## UI/UX Prototype

The fundamental user experience design reveals three essential stages which users need to complete 
when they search for locations and check accessible workspace features before they can book or assess spaces.
The core user journey begins with users finding locations and they proceed to detailed workspace features and finally 
they reach the booking and review interface.
